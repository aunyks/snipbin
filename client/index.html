Immutable

Although you can make as many changes as you want, snippet history is never deleted.

Your Information, Undamaged

Rest assured that Snipbin's blockchain backend makes it nearly impossible for someone else to edit your snippets.

Your Snippets are Yours

Each Ethereum address can have one snippet at a time.


You can share your snipbin.co/[address] link with anyone so that they can see your snippets! Each one is public.

Don't Worry

Trying to send a message to a friend? Snipbin snippets are very secure from attack.

Collect Funds

Just send somebody your Snipbin link and your address will appear alongside your snippets. This lets anybody tip you for your snippets if they choose!


Get Started Now:

  1. Make sure you're in a Web3 enabled browser like Chrome with MetaMask, Brave, or Mist.
  2. Click the "Create / Edit" button at the top of the page or go to snipbin.co/edit.
  3. Type what you want to be in your text snippet.
  4. Save your snippet by clicking the "Submit" button and paying the transaction fee.
  5. When the transaction is confirmed, go to snipbin.co/[your address] and check out your snippet!
